Welcome to HerO

HerO is the IoT plaform for Bridgetek's microcontrollers including FT9xx series. It allows secure remote access and control of MCU-based smart device.

With HerO, you'll easily be able to control MCU components such as GPIOs, RTC, Network IP, etc. and including volume, brightness, color, temperature, humidity and etc.

IoT made easy and friendly

With HerO, IoT devices and smart things becomes easy (for manufacturers) and friendly (for consumers)!

Manufacturers often need to create their own mobile apps and backend cloud system to control their smart devices.

Consumers don't want to have different apps for different brands of smart devices. Imagine if you have Xiaomi smart bulbs and TP-Link smart plugs, you'll need to download both YeeLight and Kasa mobile apps. What if you have more brands? Then you'll have more apps to download and manage.
